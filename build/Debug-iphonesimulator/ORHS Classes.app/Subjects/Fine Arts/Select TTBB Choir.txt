Class Name: Select TTBB Choir
Subject/Category: Fine Arts
Number of Credits: 1.0
GPA: 4.0
Average Number of Hours of Homework per Day: NA
Description: This class is open to intermediate to advanced tenor or bass voiced singers by audition or director signature. The curriculum emphasizes sight reading, music theory, music history and vocal performance skills using a wide variety of musical styles. Some after school rehearsals and performances are required.
